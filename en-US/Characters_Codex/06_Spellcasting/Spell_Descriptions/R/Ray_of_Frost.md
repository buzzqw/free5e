_Evocation cantrip_

**Casting Time:** 1 action \
**Range:** 60 feet \
**Components:** V, S \
**Duration:** Instantaneous

An icy beam shoots from your outstretched fingers.
Make a ranged spell attack against one creature.
On a hit, you deal `1d8` cold damage and reduce the target’s speed by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.

This spell’s damage increases by `1d8` when you reach 5th level (`2d8`), 11th level (`3d8`), and 17th level (`4d8`).
