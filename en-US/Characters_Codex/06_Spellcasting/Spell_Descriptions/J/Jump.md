_1st-level transmutation_

**Casting Time:** 1 action \
**Range:** Touch \
**Components:** V, S, M (a grasshopper’s hind leg) \
**Duration:** 1 minute

You touch a creature.
The creature’s [jump distance](#Exploration_Movement_jumping) is tripled until the spell ends.
